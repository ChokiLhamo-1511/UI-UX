Dorji Trozey Restaurant

The Problem

Diners in Bhutan struggle to find restaurants that offer a seamless experience—from easy table reservations to transparent menus and reliable service. Many face issues like last-minute unavailability, unclear pricing, and lack of personalized dining options. They need a restaurant that combines convenience, quality, and ambiance for both everyday dining and special occasions.

The Product

Dorji Trozey Restaurant is a modern dining destination that enhances the restaurant experience through digital innovation. By integrating online table reservations, an interactive menu with photos and prices, and reliable service for dine-in. Dorji Trozey aims to make dining effortless, enjoyable, and memorable.

The Goal

To provide a seamless and delightful dining experience by simplifying table bookings, enhancing menu transparency, and offering high-quality service. Whether for a quick lunch, a business dinner, or a celebration, Dorji Trozey ensures every customer’s expectations are met with efficiency and warmth.

Project Duration

March 2024 - June 2024




User Research

To better understand post-order user experience, I conducted moderated usability interviews with diners at Dorji Trozey Restaurant.

Participants highlighted frustrations around the lack of real-time table availability and delayed order readiness for takeaway. Many expressed the need for instant booking confirmations and clearer order status updates.

These insights revealed the need for a streamlined reservation system, real-time order tracking, and prompt communication to enhance convenience and build trust in Dorji Trozey’s dining experience.

Key Pain Points Identified

From the research, we identified four major user pain points:

Booking Frustrations

Guests often struggle with last-minute table unavailability and no online reservation system.

Service Delays

Slow service during peak hours and poor coordination for takeaway orders affect the customer experience.

Unclear Menu Information

Lack of transparent pricing, food photos, and dietary labels creates confusion and dissatisfaction.

Lack of Personalized Options

There are limited offerings for group bookings, special occasions, which discourages celebrations and repeat visits.

User Flow

The user flow at Dorji Trozey Restaurant offers a smooth and convenient dining experience. Customers can easily browse a variety of dishes, place orders for dine-in or takeaway, and reserve tables by selecting the date, time, and number of guests. The process concludes with a secure checkout, supporting multiple payment options and instant confirmation of orders and reservations.

Usability Study Finding

Usability testing was conducted with diners to evaluate the restaurant’s digital services, including table reservations and takeaway ordering.

Unclear Reservation Status: Users were unsure whether their table booking was successful due to vague or delayed confirmations.

Desire for Notification System: Participants wanted real-time notifications (via SMS) for booking confirmations and order readiness.

MockUp

Based on the usability study, users were often unsure whether their table reservation was successful due to vague or delayed confirmations. To address this, I implemented a dedicated reservation confirmation screen that clearly displays a success message, reservation details, and an option to modify or cancel the booking.

Accessibility Considerations

Form Labels and Instructions

All form fields for ordering and table reservations—such as meal customization options, date/time selectors, and guest number inputs—are clearly labeled with descriptive text and helpful instructions to assist users with cognitive impairments or those using screen readers.

Focus Indicators

Interactive elements like menu buttons, reservation controls, and checkout fields have visible focus states, ensuring that keyboard-only users can easily track their position while navigating the site.

Consistent Navigation

A consistent and straightforward navigation layout is maintained throughout the platform, allowing users to effortlessly browse the menu, place orders, and reserve tables without confusion, benefiting users with cognitive challenges.

Conclusion

The Dorji Trozey Restaurant platform was designed to create a seamless and accessible digital experience that reflects the warmth and quality of the restaurant itself. Through thoughtful layout, intuitive navigation, and inclusive accessibility features, the platform caters to a wide range of users. From browsing the menu to making reservations, every interaction was crafted to be user-friendly and engaging. This project not only enhances the restaurant's digital presence but also sets the stage for future improvements based on user feedback and evolving customer needs.
