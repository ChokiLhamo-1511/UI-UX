Gadget Store

The Problem

Tech shoppers in Bhutan struggle to find affordable, high-quality gadgets with reliable after-sales support. They need a trustworthy platform offering genuine products, fast delivery, and excellent customer service.

The Product
What We Offer:

GadgetStore is a mobile platform that simplifies the tech shopping experience. We provide a user-friendly app that helps shoppers find quality gadgets at affordable prices, with fast delivery and excellent support.

The Goal

Our platform makes it easy for everyone to discover and purchase the latest tech and gadgets. With a user-friendly interface, competitive prices, and reliable delivery, we simplify the tech shopping experience.

Project Duration

March 2024 - June 2024




User Research

For user research, I conducted interviews with Bhutanese gadget buyers to understand their shopping behaviors and preferences. I initially assumed that product variety and pricing were the main concerns, but discovered that users prioritized smooth payment processes, fast OTP verification, and real-time order tracking. These insights will guide the design of the Gadget Store platform to focus on reliability, instant confirmation, and transparent delivery updates for a more seamless and trustworthy shopping experience.

Key Pain Points Identified

Addressing these challenges became our design priorities

Limited Availability

Users struggle to find latest gadgets locally, facing long shipping times and high fees from international sites.

Authenticity Concerns

Users fear fake/refurbished gadgets and question seller reliability during checkout.

Unclear Specifications

Incomplete product info makes it hard to compare and select gadgets confidently.

Delivery Uncertainty

Vague timelines and lack of live tracking leave users unsure about order status.

Usability Study Finding

To better understand post-purchase user experience, I conducted moderated usability interviews with Bhutanese gadget buyers.

Unclear Payment Confirmation: Users were often unsure if their payment was successful due to the absence of a prominent confirmation message.

OTP Delays: Several participants reported delays in receiving One-Time Passwords (OTPs), causing frustration and failed transactions.

MockUp

To resolve this, a dedicated payment success page was designed, featuring a clear confirmation message, order summary, and tracking options.

Accessibility Considerations

Form Labels and Instructions

Clearly labeled all form fields, such as search bars, login inputs, and checkout forms, with descriptive text and inline instructions to assist users with cognitive impairments or screen reader reliance.

Focus Indicators

Ensured all interactive elements like buttons, links, and form fields have visible focus states. This helps keyboard-only users know where they are on the page while navigating.

Consistent Navigation

Maintained a consistent navigation structure throughout the site, helping users quickly learn how to move between sections and find products without confusion, which is especially beneficial for users with cognitive disabilities.

Conclusion

The Gadget Store mobile app aims to redefine tech shopping in Bhutan by addressing user pain points like authenticity concerns, unclear delivery, and payment insecurity. Through iterative design and usability testing, features like real-time order tracking, secure OTP verification, and clear product specifications were refined. The platform offers a smooth and trustworthy shopping experience, tailored for local users.
